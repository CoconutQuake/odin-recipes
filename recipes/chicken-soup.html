Ingredients

  • A LIVE chicken
  • Chicken broth
  • Eye of newt
  • Crimson nirnroot
  • Expensive truffle shavings
  • Celery
  • Whatever else was in the photo

Steps:

  1. Realize that harming this chicken will literally traumatize you and let it play with your cat instead.
  2. Pour the broth into your roommate's wok because you still don't own a pot.
  3. Chop the celery and then compost it because you hate celery.
  4. Snack on the truffle shavings while you cook
  5. Throw the eye of newt and remaning ingredients into the pot.
  6. Simmer for several hours.
  7. Use your roommate's spatula to taste your broth and add salt if needed.
  8. Pour into coffee mugs and enjoy! Serves 13
